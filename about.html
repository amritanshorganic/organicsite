About Us

We, Amritansh Organic Pvt. Ltd. (AOPL) works with Organic Certified Farmer Groups (OCFGs)/FPOs and share know how of organic farming and innovative and hitech techniques of farming that helps to increase their farm yield as well as their income. Since long back We help farmers for market linkage that help to get their crop better price.

Since long back Amritansh Organic had been working on facilitating linkages between organic producers and traders, In the year 2022 it set up its marketing arm to facilitate direct linkage between the consumer and producers.

We directly source material from farmers from various agro-climatic zones that extends our outreach and also increases our product range, within the last year, while Amritansh Organic has not only been able to establish in-house as well as contracted processing facilities, it has set up its own distribution and retail network in India.
We started our journey with cereals, pulses, oil seeds and spices, today Amritansh Organic has portfolio of over 190 different products. We supply products in bulk and also encourage new entrepreneurs to come into organic food business to help them for private labeling.
